Not a Course Catalog System

Common misconception: "Pillar II courses teach RF, Pillar III courses teach fabrication."

Reality: Every course touches multiple pillars. FFF-401 covers all five. FFR-201 emphasizes Pillar II but includes Pillar I platform knowledge and Pillar III field antenna procedures. The pillars are a training methodology — not a filing system.

What Makes Us Different

Three structural advantages no standalone training organization can offer.

Integrated Enterprise

The only training organization connected to an enterprise that designs and manufactures UAS platforms, produces NDAA-compliant avionics, and operates dedicated test ranges. Train on systems drawn from real production and validation operations — not generic consumer hardware.

Operator Self-Sufficiency

Training emphasizes field sustainment and organic repair capability. Reduce contractor dependency. Enable rapid recovery after platform attrition. Maintain operational readiness with degraded supply chains and minimal external support.

Five-Pillar Framework

Comprehensive training methodology spanning unmanned systems, RF/spectrum, field fabrication, autonomy/AI, and capability assessment. Integrated competency development — not a disconnected list of single-skill courses.

Future-Ready Curriculum

Built on Ukrainian operational lessons learned and current SOCOM requirements. AI integration, spectrum warfare, and autonomous systems from day one — not legacy approaches.

Veteran-Led Instruction

All instructors are FAA-certified remote pilots with real operational experience. Regulatory knowledge and tactical expertise — not academic theory delivered without operational context.

NDAA Compliance

All training platforms use American-made avionics manufactured within the Holdings enterprise, fully compliant with NDAA Section 848. No Chinese-manufactured components in any platform used during training.

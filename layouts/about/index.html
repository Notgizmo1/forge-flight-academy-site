What Makes Us Different

Three structural advantages no standalone training organization can offer.

Integrated Enterprise

The only training organization connected to an enterprise that designs and manufactures UAS platforms, produces NDAA-compliant avionics, and operates dedicated test ranges. Train on systems drawn from real production and validation operations — not generic consumer hardware.

Operator Self-Sufficiency

Training emphasizes field sustainment and organic repair capability. Reduce contractor dependency. Enable rapid recovery after platform attrition. Maintain operational readiness with degraded supply chains.

Five-Pillar Framework

Comprehensive training methodology spanning unmanned systems, RF/spectrum, field fabrication, autonomy/AI, and capability assessment. Integrated competency development — not a disconnected list of single-skill courses.

Veteran-Led Instruction

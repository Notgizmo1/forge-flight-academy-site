Pre-Flight Checklist

A comprehensive checklist for safe, compliant Part 107 operations. Use before every flight to ensure readiness.

How to Use This Checklist

✓ Review all sections before each flight
✓ Check off items as completed
✓ Address any issues before launching
✓ When in doubt, don’t fly

Download Printable PDF

1. Regulatory Compliance

Remote Pilot Certificate

☐ Current Part 107 certificate in possession
☐ Not expired (valid 24 months from issuance)
☐ Recurrent training completed if renewal due

Aircraft Registration

☐ Drone registered with FAA (if 0.55 - 55 lbs)
☐ Registration number displayed on aircraft
☐ Registration current (3-year validity)

Airspace Authorization

☐ Checked for Temporary Flight Restrictions (TFRs)
☐ LAANC authorization obtained (if required)
☐ ATC coordination completed (if manual authorization)
☐ NOTAMs reviewed for area

Operating Rules

☐ Flight will comply with Part 107 regulations
☐ Special waivers in place (if needed)
☐ Operating limitations understood

2. Personal Fitness - I’M SAFE

I - Illness

☐ Feeling healthy and alert
☐ No cold, flu, or illness symptoms
☐ No impairments affecting judgment

M - Medication

☐ Not taking impairing medications
☐ No prescription drugs affecting performance
☐ No over-the-counter drugs causing drowsiness

S - Stress

☐ Mentally prepared for flight
☐ No personal issues affecting focus
☐ Able to concentrate on operation

A - Alcohol

☐ No alcohol consumption in previous 8 hours
☐ Blood alcohol content below 0.04%
☐ Not suffering from hangover effects

F - Fatigue

☐ Well rested (6-8 hours sleep minimum)
☐ Alert and focused
☐ No drowsiness or exhaustion

E - Emotion

☐ Emotionally stable
☐ Able to make sound decisions
☐ Not distracted by personal matters

If any I’M SAFE item is checked “NO” - DO NOT FLY

3. Weather Assessment

Current Conditions

☐ METAR reviewed for nearest airport
☐ Visibility ≥ 3 statute miles
☐ Cloud clearance: 500 ft below, 2,000 ft horizontal
☐ Visual Meteorological Conditions (VMC) present

Wind Conditions

☐ Wind speed within aircraft limitations
☐ Wind direction noted: _________
☐ Gusts considered: _________ kt
☐ Crosswind component acceptable

Environmental Factors

☐ Temperature acceptable for batteries
☐ No precipitation affecting operation
☐ Density altitude calculated (if hot/high altitude)
☐ No thunderstorms in vicinity

Forecast

☐ TAF reviewed (if available)
☐ No adverse weather expected during flight
☐ Forecast conditions suitable for mission

4. Aircraft Inspection

Airframe Visual Check

☐ No cracks or damage to body
☐ Props free of damage or chips
☐ Landing gear secure
☐ No loose screws or parts
☐ Registration marking visible

Propellers

☐ All propellers present and undamaged
☐ Props mounted securely
☐ No cracks or missing pieces
☐ Prop guards installed (if applicable)

Sensors & Systems

☐ Vision sensors clean
☐ Obstacle avoidance unobstructed
☐ GPS antenna undamaged
☐ Gimbal moves freely (if applicable)
☐ Camera lens clean

Battery Check

☐ Main battery fully charged: ____%
☐ No physical damage or swelling
☐ Properly seated in aircraft
☐ Terminals clean
☐ Temperature acceptable (not too hot/cold)

Controller

☐ Controller battery charged: ____%
☐ Controller paired with aircraft
☐ Spare batteries available
☐ Control sticks move freely

5. Systems Check

Control Links

☐ Aircraft responds to pitch input
☐ Aircraft responds to roll input
☐ Aircraft responds to yaw input
☐ Aircraft responds to throttle input
☐ No control lag or delay

GPS & Navigation

☐ GPS satellites acquired: _____ (min 6-8)
☐ GPS signal strong
☐ Home point set correctly
☐ Compass calibration recent
☐ No magnetic interference

Flight Systems

☐ Return-to-Home (RTH) altitude set: _____ ft
☐ RTH function tested
☐ Battery return threshold set: ____%
☐ Firmware up to date
☐ No system warnings or errors

6. Mission Planning

Flight Area

☐ Site surveyed for hazards
☐ Obstacles identified and noted
☐ Emergency landing zones identified
☐ No people in immediate flight area
☐ Property owner permission obtained (if required)

Flight Parameters

☐ Maximum altitude: _____ ft AGL (≤400 ft)
☐ Flight area boundaries defined
☐ Estimated flight time: _____ min
☐ Sufficient battery for mission + reserve

Communication

☐ Visual observer briefed (if used)
☐ Helper/spotter briefed on duties
☐ Emergency procedures reviewed
☐ Lost link procedures established

Special Requirements

☐ Camera settings configured
☐ Flight modes selected
☐ Autonomous mission uploaded (if applicable)
☐ Waypoints verified

7. Safety Measures

Equipment

☐ First aid kit available
☐ Fire extinguisher (if needed)
☐ Spare parts/tools available
☐ Landing pad deployed (if applicable)

Documentation

☐ Remote pilot certificate
☐ Aircraft registration
☐ Airspace authorization (printed/digital)
☐ Insurance documentation (if applicable)

Notifications

☐ Airport notified (if within 5 NM of towered airport)
☐ Local authorities notified (if required)
☐ Participants/public informed
☐ Barriers established (if operations near people)

8. Pre-Launch Operations

Final Checks

☐ Area clear of people and vehicles
☐ Launch area safe and level
☐ Wind direction confirmed
☐ Camera/gimbal functioning

Launch Sequence

☐ Controller in correct mode
☐ Aircraft responding to all controls
☐ Hover test at 3-5 ft completed
☐ All systems showing normal status

In-Flight Monitoring

☐ Visual line of sight maintained
☐ Battery level monitored continuously
☐ Weather conditions monitored
☐ Airspace awareness maintained
☐ People/obstacles monitored

Abort Criteria

DO NOT FLY IF:

❌ Weather below Part 107 minimums
❌ Personal fitness compromised (I’M SAFE)
❌ Aircraft showing mechanical issues
❌ Airspace authorization not obtained
❌ Active TFR in effect
❌ Any safety concerns present

When in doubt, don’t fly!

Post-Flight Procedures

Aircraft Inspection

☐ No damage from flight
☐ All parts accounted for
☐ Props checked for damage
☐ Aircraft cleaned (if necessary)

Battery Management

☐ Battery removed from aircraft
☐ Battery allowed to cool before storage
☐ Battery stored at 40-60% charge

Documentation

☐ Flight time logged
☐ Any incidents/issues documented
☐ Required reports filed (if applicable)
☐ Data/footage backed up

Additional Notes

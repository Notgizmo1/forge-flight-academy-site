FFF-402: FPV Master Trainer Certification

FFF-402

FPV Master Trainer Certification

Become a certified FPV instructor. Train-the-trainer program for organizations establishing sustainable internal training capability without perpetual dependence on commercial training providers.

Program Overview

This program qualifies experienced operators to train others to FPV Tactical Operator Level 1 standards. Training covers instructional methodology, curriculum delivery, safety management, and student assessment.

Key Focus Areas:

  • Instructional methodology and adult learning principles
  • Curriculum structure and lesson planning
  • Student assessment and proficiency evaluation
  • Safety management and risk mitigation
  • Training documentation and standards maintenance
  • Quality assurance and instructor certification

Who Should Attend

Primary Audience:

  • Experienced FPV operators (500+ flight hours)
  • Unit training NCOs and officers
  • Organizations establishing internal training programs
  • Prime contractors supporting system fielding
  • Research institutions developing training capability

Prerequisites: FFF-401 completion or equivalent experience, demonstrated FPV proficiency

Training Approach

Progressive responsibility from assistant instructor to lead instructor. Students teach actual FFF-401 curriculum to new operators under supervision, receiving feedback and coaching.

Program Outcomes

Upon completion, instructors demonstrate capability to:

  • Deliver complete FFF-401 curriculum independently
  • Assess student proficiency using standardized criteria
  • Manage training safety and risk
  • Maintain instructional quality and standards
  • Certify new operators to Level 1 standards

Certification and Licensing

Graduates receive FPV Master Trainer Certificate and curriculum license authorizing instruction of FFF-401 content within their organization. Annual recertification required.

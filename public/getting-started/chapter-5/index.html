Building Experience

Practice recommendations:

  • Aim for 50+ flight hours in first year
  • Fly in diverse conditions and locations
  • Practice different times of day
  • Vary weather conditions (within limits)

Join communities:

  • Local AMA flying clubs
  • Drone racing leagues
  • Professional associations
  • Online forums

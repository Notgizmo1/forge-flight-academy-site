University & Research Institution Training

Hands-on UAS training for academic research programs, aerospace engineering curricula, and university laboratories.

We provide capability-based operator training and assessment services that complement academic theory with demonstrable, hands-on proficiency.

Training Services for Universities

1. Student Operator Training

Hands-on instruction preparing students to safely operate research platforms

Part 107 Preparation

  • Comprehensive exam prep course
  • Regulatory knowledge for academic research
  • Airspace authorization procedures
  • Certificate of Aeronautical Knowledge (COA) process

Platform-Specific Training

  • Fixed-wing UAS operations
  • Multi-rotor operations (quadcopters, hexacopters)
  • VTOL (vertical takeoff and landing) platforms
  • Custom research platforms

Laboratory Safety & Operations

  • Pre-flight inspection and risk assessment
  • Flight operations in controlled environments
  • Emergency procedures and incident response
  • Data collection and flight logging

Outcome: Students gain FAA Part 107 knowledge + hands-on proficiency for research operations.

2. Faculty & Staff Development

Training research staff to safely oversee student-operated UAS programs

  • UAS program management and safety oversight
  • Capability assessment methodologies
  • Risk mitigation for research operations
  • Grant writing for UAS-related research (NSF, DoD, NASA)

Outcome: Faculty can confidently supervise student research while maintaining safety standards.

3. Curriculum Integration Services

We help universities build UAS components into existing programs

Services include:

  • Course development for aerospace, robotics, or engineering programs
  • Lab exercise design with hands-on learning objectives
  • Assessment rubrics tied to ABET or program learning outcomes
  • Equipment recommendations for academic budgets

4. Research Platform Development

Build and configure custom platforms for academic research

We assist with:

  • Platform selection for research objectives
  • Sensor integration (LiDAR, multi-spectral, thermal, etc.)
  • Flight controller configuration (ArduPilot, PX4, custom)
  • Data collection systems and onboard processing
  • Field testing support for experimental platforms

Outcome: Researchers have reliable, well-configured platforms ready for data collection.

5. Capability Assessment & Certification

Independent evaluation of student or staff UAS proficiency

We provide:

  • 1-5 capability rating across demonstrable skill domains
  • Documented assessment reports for academic records
  • Skills gap analysis for training progression
  • Certification exam proctoring (Part 107, manufacturer certs)

Use Cases:

  • Capstone project assessment
  • Graduate research assistant qualification
  • Lab safety certification requirements
  • Pre-deployment validation for field research

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do you offer semester-long courses?
A: We typically deliver intensive multi-day courses, but we can consult on semester-long curriculum design for your faculty to teach. Or internship type programs

Q: Can students keep the aircraft they build?
A: For our flagship course, yes. For custom programs, we can structure options for student-owned platforms or university-owned lab equipment.

Q: What if our students already have Part 107 certificates?
A: Great! Our training focuses on hands-on proficiency beyond the exam. We can skip Part 107 prep and focus on platform operations and assessment.

Q: Can you train international students?
A: Yes, for non-ITAR controlled content. Export-controlled training requires proper authorization.

Q: Do you provide equipment for university labs?
A: We can recommend equipment and suppliers for academic budgets. We don’t sell equipment directly, but we can connect you with vendors offering educational discounts.

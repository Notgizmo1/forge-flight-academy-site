### Professional Development

#### Part 107 Certification

**For commercial operations** - obtain FAA Part 107 Remote Pilot Certificate.

**Benefits:**
- Legal commercial operations
- Enhanced credibility
- Access to advanced opportunities
- Professional liability coverage options

[View Part 107 Study Guide →](/resources/part-107-study-guide/)  
[Take Practice Test →](/resources/practice-test/)

#### Specialized Training

**Professional programs** develop specific capabilities:

- **FPV Operations** - Tactical training, racing, advanced maneuvers
- **Inspection & Survey** - Infrastructure inspection, mapping, photogrammetry
- **Advanced Autonomy** - Mission planning, multi-aircraft operations
- **Field Sustainment** - Assembly, repair, electronics troubleshooting

[View All Training Programs →](/courses/)

---

### Building Experience

**Practice recommendations:**
- Aim for 50+ flight hours in first year
- Fly in diverse conditions and locations
- Practice different times of day
- Vary weather conditions (within limits)

**Join communities:**
- Local AMA flying clubs
- Drone racing leagues
- Professional associations
- Online forums

### Professional Training Programs

**Forge and Flight Academy** offers comprehensive training across five capability pillars:

1. **Unmanned & Autonomous Systems** - Operations, assembly, maintenance
2. **RF, Spectrum & Communications** - EW countermeasures, link management
3. **Field Fabrication & Sustainment** - Electronics repair, 3D printing
4. **Autonomy, AI & Edge Computing** - Advanced autonomy, mission planning
5. **Capability Assessment** - Proficiency evaluation, unit readiness

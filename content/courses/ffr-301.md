## Advanced Electronic Warfare for UAS Operations

**Advanced EW countermeasures and spectrum operations planning.** Integration with unit electromagnetic warfare officers and sophisticated counter-EW tactics for contested operational environments.

### Program Overview

This program develops advanced electronic warfare capabilities for UAS operations. Training covers sophisticated countermeasures, spectrum deconfliction, and coordination with S-6/G-6 electromagnetic warfare elements.

**Key Focus Areas:**
- Advanced jamming countermeasures
- Spectrum operations planning and coordination
- Counter-UAS tactics and techniques
- SIGINT and communications intelligence
- Integration with electromagnetic warfare officers
- Operational security in contested spectrum

### Who Should Attend

**Primary Audience:**
- Advanced UAS operators in EW-intensive environments
- Unit electromagnetic warfare coordinators
- Special operations UAS elements
- Intelligence personnel focusing on EW threats
- Training cadre developing EW curriculum

**Prerequisites:** FFR-201 completion or equivalent RF experience

### Training Approach

Advanced techniques beyond basic RF troubleshooting. Integration with actual S-6/G-6 electromagnetic warfare planning processes and coordination procedures.

### Program Outcomes

Upon completion, operators demonstrate capability to:
- Implement advanced EW countermeasures
- Plan UAS operations in coordination with EW officers
- Assess and mitigate sophisticated EW threats
- Integrate UAS operations into spectrum management plans
- Support counter-UAS operations from EW perspective
